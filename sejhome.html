Know Thyself to Love Thyself


Welcome!

How do you define yourself? Why do you feel the way you do? Have you thought about it recently, or at all?

Self-Awareness, or introspection, is one of the most important skills you can have. Self-Awareness simply means, learning about yourself and asking those hard to ask questions. When you learn about yourself you figure out why you do things that you do, and feel the way that you do. Self-Improvement and self-control are impossible without self-awarenes. Self-Awareness is the major component of Emotional Intelligence (ability to to identify and manage self and others' emotions. Self-Awareness leads to happier people who have a positive outlook on life, are in good psychological health, and act consciously instead of passively. If you check out the Readings below you will find ways to improve self-awareness and why it is difficult to obtain.

Self-Esteem can be the difference between sucess and failure by affecting your thinking in a positive or negative outlook. Your confidence, self-image, happiness and attitude are all affected by self-esteem. Low self-esteem is detrimental to success because when told work, thoughts, or behaviors are not right the individual will believe that perpetuating the low self-esteem. How do you succeed if you believe everyone when they tell you you are wrong? Self-esteem, luckily, can be changed. It takes work but it can be done!
